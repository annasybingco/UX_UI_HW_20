Project Overview

The project was to create a fully clickable prototype for any mobile application. I created Oui Chef meant to establish more confident home cooks. By giving them instructional videos, whether they are beginners or pros any home cook would benefit from Oui Chef.

The Problem

Cooking at home is a time-consuming and tedious process that comes with its own set of worries. How might we make the process of cooking at home easier and more streamlined?

The Solution

Oui Chef introduces users to recipes based on their time constraints with voice command instructional videos led by prof chefs. You can play, pause or rewind recipes via voice command. Voice recognition means no more touching your screen with dirty fingers!

Research

Interview Plan: We conducted 5 interviews in order to find what steps users follow when cooking at home and what are their limitations. We discovered a common theme between our interviews; that users are eager to cook at home but find cooking to be time consuming. A survey of 130 participants between the ages of 18-38 affirmed our stance with the majority voting that they enjoy cooking at home but are conflicted when finding inspiration for new recipes.
